from : speedcamerapoi.com

Key
X – longitude coordinate
Y – latitude coordinate

TYPE – Camera type: 
1 – Speed camera, 
3 – Red Light camera, 
5 – Mobile police camera/radar

SPEED – Speed limit: 0,20,30,40,50,60,70,80,90,100,110,120

DIRTYPE – indicates which direction the camera works in:
0 – any direction, 
1 – only direction of travel, 
2 – both directions

DIRECTION – Camera direction in degrees, stored to the nearest 45º
